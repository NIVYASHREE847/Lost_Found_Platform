Privacy Policy — Found Lost

Last updated: DD/MM/YYYY

Found Lost (“we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and your choices regarding your information when you use the Found Lost website and mobile applications (the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, password.
  • Item details: descriptions, photos, serial numbers, and other information you submit about lost or found items.
  • User communications: messages exchanged via the platform, support requests, and feedback.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device & usage data: device model, OS, browser, pages viewed, interaction data, and timestamps.
  • Location data: approximate location when you enable location services to help locate items.
  • Log & diagnostic data: crash reports and performance analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service.
  • To help locate lost items and match found items with potential owners.
  • To communicate with you (notifications, support, updates).
  • To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud or illegal activity.
  • To analyze usage and improve user experience.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other Users: when you post an item or respond to a post, certain item details and your username/contact info (if you choose) may be visible to others.
  • Service providers: third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • Legal requirements: when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Your Choices

  • Account settings: update or delete your account information in settings.
  • Location: you can enable or disable location sharing; disabling may reduce some functionality.
  • Communications: opt out of non-essential emails or notifications via account preferences.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you delete your account we will remove or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal or operational requirements.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure — we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Third-Party Links & Services

The Service may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their privacy policies before sharing information.

9. International Transfers

Information collected may be stored and processed in servers located in India or other countries. By using the Service you consent to transfer of information to countries that may have different data protection laws.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
